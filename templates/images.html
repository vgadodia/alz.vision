Health-Based, Personalized Recommendations

Our API takes into account both the user's tastes and health needs when making recommendations. All suggested recipes are carefully picked to help our users consume the right amounts of fat, protein, and fiber based on their previous food intake on the same day.

Powered by Machine Learning

On the backend, our recommendation system uses a word vector clustering algorithm to predict which ingredients the user will like based on their past meals. After taking nutritional values into account, we recommend a recipe with ingredients the algorithm predicts the user will enjoy.
